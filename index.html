FAQ

You Ask, We Build

How durable are the models?

We choose materials for the job: tough resins for structure, nylon SLS for load, and multi‑material FDM for functional parts.

Do you do custom paint?

Yes. We color‑match to codes or samples and offer gloss, satin, matte, and carbon‑look finishes.

Can you work from sketches?

Absolutely. We lift geometry from sketches, photos, or rough CAD and iterate with you.

Shipping & lead times?

Typical builds ship 2–6 weeks depending on complexity and finishing. Rush options available.
